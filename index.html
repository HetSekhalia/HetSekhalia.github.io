About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineering master’s student at Carnegie Mellon University specializing in control systems, autonomous robotics, and deep reinforcement learning. My work focuses on building reliable, real-time autonomy stacks for complex robotic systems—ranging from heavy construction equipment to simulated autonomous vehicles—using motion planning, feedback control, and machine learning. Across both research and project work, I aim to design systems that are safe, efficient, and deployable in real-world conditions. I am particularly interested in advancing intelligent robotics that improve productivity and safety in industrial environments.
